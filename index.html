Module 01

VeriPath Crops

Every flagged record, expired certificate, or EUDR risk costs you a delayed shipment and demurrage fees at port. VeriPath catches all three before your consignment ever leaves the farm.

  • Quarantine flagged records before they reach the port — not after a shipment's already rejected
  • Replace paper log books and manual re-entry — one QR scan in the field records packhouse intake digitally, instantly
  • Every certificate verified and cross-checked automatically — no compliance officer needed to catch a fraudulent or expired document
  • Know your EUDR risk before inspectors do — avoid a shipment blocked at the last minute
  • One-click submission to KenTrade and KEPHIS — cut the hours your team spends on manual portal entry
  • Satellite crop health monitoring flags a stressed field weeks before it becomes a bad harvest
  • 48-hour flash weather alerts protect your season before a storm costs you the crop
  • Compliance documents generated automatically for every batch — no staff time spent assembling paperwork

Module 02

VeriPath Livestock

One undetected fever can spread through a herd before you notice a single animal acting off. VeriPath catches it days earlier — before it's a vet bill, and before it's an outbreak.

  • Catch a sick animal days before symptoms show — before it spreads and costs you the herd
  • GREEN / YELLOW / RED status tells real fever from normal heat — no unnecessary vet call-outs
  • A RED alert dispatches a vet by WhatsApp instantly — faster response, fewer losses
  • Early warning scoring for CCPP, PPR and ECF, automatically, without a daily manual check
  • Full animal registry replaces the paper notebook — no lost records, no admin overhead
